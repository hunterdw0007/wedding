Happy Couple

Hunter

the Groom

I love being with Mauren because I'm always around someone who makes me want to be a better person, in every sense of the word. I've done so many new things since we've been together that I don't think I would have ever done otherwise: moving to New Mexico, snowboarding, traveling, and adventuring. But even if we aren't doing exciting things, we still love to spend our time together playing video games, watching movies, reading, or cooking. We genuinely love spending time together and never get tired of each other's company. I'm so happy I saw Mauren in Dr. Qin's Operating Systems class at Auburn and decided to say hello.

Mauren

the Bride

Shortly after beginning my degree path at Auburn I had convinved myself that there was no way I was going to start a relationship with anyone else in computer science or software engineering. Most of the population was a bit nerdy, and I worried that daily conversations would solely consist of tech-related nonsense. Having been with Hunter for over 3 years now, I can confirm that my thoughts were true, in part, but I wouldn't have it any other way. There is true comfort in coming home from a long, frusterating day of work and being to able to vent to a person who knows exactly what you are talking about. As nerdy as we may be, I do brag a bit in the fact that I found someone who is a bit more well rounded in his skills and interests than the stereotypical computer whiz. "A match made in heaven" is often a cliche saying, but I thank God often for the blessing of being truly loved and truly known by Hunter and hope that he always feels the same.
